INTRODUCTION

Summary

Henry Huynh is a User Experience Designer specializing in bespoke, artisanal, single origin, grass fed, fixed gear, and slow roasted apps & websites for clients of all sizes. Currently a Principal Experience Architect at projekt202, Jared is capable within a wide range of tactical and big-picture design tasks. Henry is passionate about making sure that systems, interfaces, language and graphics are human-friendly, emotive, aesthetically pleasing, clear, on-brand and usable — with a touch of mellow smoothness. He has worked in a variety of environments, from mega-consultancies to small-ish agencies to product companies. From workflow diagrams to detailed user interface mockups, Henry loves shaping and crafting on-screen experiences.
